Complicated

by Avril Lavigne




“Complicated” is the first single from Avril’s debut album Let Go, and
by extension her entire music career. It’s about the problems that arise
when you pretend to be something you’re not and you just need to chill, relax, and enjoy life.

The song held the top spot on Billboard’s Adult Top 40 chart for 16
weeks in a row and reached the #2 spot on the Hot 100. The song
went on to sell over 3 million copies worldwide.

Reference




Avril Lavigne (born September 27, 1984, Belleville, Ontario, Canada)
Canadian singer and songwriter who achieved great success as a teenager.
She was known for a grungy pop-rock sound.
